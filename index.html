Who are we ?

  • We are one of the reputed speech and hearing centres in the Delhi NCR.
  • We ensure that no child with special needs remains laggard in life.
  • We have experts to help the child in sessions slowly come out shackles and be at par with other kids in the normal school.
  • Our comprehensive services improve the speech of a child, help the child gradually develop social skills, and realize the potential.

    • Features

    Speech Therapy

    Our speech therapists sit with the child in a highly relaxing ambience and practice with the child the therapy. The child learns from the expert and follows the same steps to develop fluent speech.

    Occupational Therapy

    We have the right tools, swings, mats, balls, and other such toys to teach occupational therapy to the child with fun and joy. The occupational therapy helps the child to perform the daily tasks without help from anybody and slowly develop independence.

    Special Education

    Various teaching methods are employed to meet the individual educational, cognitive, behavioral and special needs of the child. We customized the methods in one to one sessions.

    Pediatric Department

    The qualified doctors ensure that the health of each child is monitored with the best medical care, medical support and services. The department is fully equipped to handle all cases of infants, children and adolescent.

    ABA Therapy

    Applied behaviors analysts are worldwide accepted and proven therapy to help the child suffering from autism learn new positive behavior. The therapy teaches basic learning, reading, and listening and conversation skills.

    Hearing Aid Fitting

    The comprehensive hearing test is conducted in the fully equipped clinic to test the hearing range of the child before providing the best hearing aid that helps the child to deal with all situations.

    Sensory Integration

    The sensory integration is the foundation for later complex learning. It involves teaching the brain to interpret sensory experience such as touch, sound, smell, taste and pull of gravity in a play based activities.

    Vocational Training

    Our well-equipped workshop provides practical and skill-based training to help our client apply the learned skills for a better career prospect and growth. The training is given by experts from the respective fields.
